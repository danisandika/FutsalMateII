Welcome to Futsalan

In this website, you can find your friends, your team, or your rival for a futsal match. Who will top the rank this week? We are waiting for your name or your team to be on top.

About the Futsalan

The Futsalan application is an application that is used to search for futsal opponents and can select a field to place an order for a futsal game. To play futsal together, you can join the match as a team or individually. If you play with a team, of course you have to join one of the teams that you want, and build good relationships between team members.

For those of you who have a Futsal field, you can work with us to provide a place to play.
